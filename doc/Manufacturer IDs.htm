XMF Resource IDs

The MIDI Manufacturers Association issues Manufacturers MIDI System Exclusive IDs to manufacturers world-wide (except Japan). These MIDI ID numbers allow manufacturers to uniquely identify their products and provide a means for communicating product-specific information over MIDI.

The MMA maintains the database of MIDI IDs, and manufacturers are encouraged to publish their MIDI System Exclusive Formats. MMA MIDI IDs are not transferable, nor assignable by nor to any other person or entity. The MMA assignment of MIDI IDs is intended to prevent conflicts and incompatibilities.

Recently Assigned Manufacturer MIDI ID Numbers

The list of MIDI ID Numbers assigned prior to January 1996 is included in the Complete MIDI 1.0 Detailed Specification . The following list shows only those assignments since January 1996, and will be updated occasionally as new numbers are assigned. (As of January 2014)

Companies listed here as "Suspended" have not renewed their MIDI ID and may no longer be manufacturing products using that MIDI ID.
